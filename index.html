NeurIPS – Cutting-Edge AI & Machine Learning Research

NeurIPS (Conference on Neural Information Processing Systems) is one of the world’s most prestigious AI and machine learning conferences.

  • Publishes peer-reviewed, top-tier AI and machine learning research
  • Introduces state-of-the-art models and algorithms
  • Covers modern evaluation methods and benchmarks
  • Widely used to justify advanced AI/ML method selection

Journal Ranking Tools – Evaluating Research Quality

Journal ranking platforms help researchers assess the credibility, impact, and academic quality of journals.

  • Evaluate journal credibility and research impact
  • Identify high-quality journals using rankings and metrics
  • Check quartile rankings (Q1–Q4) and impact indicators
  • Support informed journal selection for citation and publication
Resources:
  • SCImago Journal Rank (Free Quartile Rankings – Q1 to Q4)
  • Journal Citation Reports (Official Impact Factors – JCR)
  • Academic Journal Guide (ABS) – Business & Management Journals

Publish or Perish – Research Impact & Citation Analysis

Publish or Perish is a bibliometric analysis tool that retrieves and analyzes citation data.

  • Analyzes citations from Google Scholar and other sources
  • Identifies influential papers and leading authors
  • Calculates citation metrics such as h-index
  • Helps detect research trends and foundational studies
Resources:
  • Publish or Perish – Official Website & Download
